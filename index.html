Who We Are

We are a group of design-driven professionals passionate about creating efficient software architecture & beautiful UI designs.

Here you can find our step-by-step software development process:

Collect Requirements

We'll schedule a call with you and document all your business requirements. We discuss to fully understand each one. Along the way we may send you several versions of the document upon which we must all agree to proceed to the next step. This step usually takes between 5-7 business days.

Design

After having all the requirements signed, we proceed to design. It means our team will come up with system architecture and mock screens based on the agreed requirements. We then discuss more and figure out what need to be done to deliver the best user interface and experience.

Build

Next, we do what we do best: Code. Usually, building the application takes 50% of total project time. As this progresses, we'll inform you as our team achieves the milestones based on the requirements so that you know what's happening on our side.

Test

We have very strict policy for testing to make sure our application is ready for the most demanding users. Every unit we built will be tested carefully by our experienced developers and the results will be shared with you.

Deliver

For a first-time client, this is the final step where we deliver the code and launch your application live on our cloud infrastructure. Software documentation will be provided. And you can ask our team anything to understand how to use it.

Maintain

As said in the previous step, we also provide hosting service for your application so that it can go live for your users to use. This comes at a very competitive price. If you need us to monitor your application on a daily basis, there will be additional costs. Normally, we'd advise our customers to find out any needs for further development and do another project with us.
